\subsection{Additional Notation}

The pairwise comparison representation $\phipc$ is given as follows. For any ranking $\sigma$ and for $i$ and $j$ in $[1,n]$ with $i \neq j$, we have
$$
\phipc_{ij}(\sigma) = 
\begin{cases}
1 & \text{if } \sigma(i) < \sigma(j),\\
0 & \text{otherwise.}
\end{cases}
$$
